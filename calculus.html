Problem of the Week

Optimization Challenge

A farmer has 200 feet of fencing and wants to enclose a rectangular area next to a river. The side along the river needs no fencing. What dimensions will maximize the enclosed area?

Let $x$ = width perpendicular to river, $y$ = length parallel to river
Constraint: $2x + y = 200$
Maximize: $A = xy$
