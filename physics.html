Physics Course Syllabus

Course Overview

Physics is the fundamental science that seeks to understand how the universe works. This course covers classical mechanics, thermodynamics, waves, electricity, magnetism, and modern physics. Students will develop problem-solving skills and gain hands-on experience through laboratory experiments.
